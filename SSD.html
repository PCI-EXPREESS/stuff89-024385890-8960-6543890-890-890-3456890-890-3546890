How to Swap M.2 SSD

Step 1

Undo any screws holding in the front pannel on your computer

Step 2

Carefuly remove the front pannel and place it in a safe area

Step 3

Locate the M.2 Cover

Step 4

Unscew the screws holding down the M.2, Cover Be careful as the SSD will pop up

Step 5

Remove the M.2 SSD

Step 6

Remove your new M.2 from the packaging

Step 7

Plug the M.2 back in

Step 8

Place the M.2 Cover

That's it!
